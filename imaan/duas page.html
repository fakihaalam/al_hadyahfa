"Supplication is the essence of worship."

Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَبِسْمِ اللَّهِ، اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِيمَا رَزَقْتَنَا وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

In the name of Allah and with Allah. O Allah, bless us in what You have provided us and protect us from the punishment of the Fire.

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said to recite this before eating for blessings. (Sunan Abu Dawood)

After Eating

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَانَا وَجَعَلَنَا مُسْلِمِينَ

Praise be to Allah who fed us and provided us drink and made us Muslims.

This dua is for gratitude to Allah after eating. (Sunan Ibn Majah)

Before Sleeping

بِاسْمِكَ رَبِّي وَضَعْتُ جَنْبِي وَبِكَ أَرْفَعُهُ

In Your name, my Lord, I lay down and in Your name I rise.

Recite this before sleeping for protection. (Sahih Bukhari)

After Waking Up

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ

Praise be to Allah who gave us life after death and to Him is the return.

Thank Allah for giving life after sleep. (Sahih Bukhari)

Entering the Home

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَ الْمَوْلَجِ وَخَيْرَ الْمَخْرَجِ

O Allah, I ask You for the best entrance and the best exit.

Seek blessings upon entering your home. (Muslim)

Leaving the Home

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللَّهِ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ

In the name of Allah, I place my trust in Allah, and there is no power nor might except with Allah.

Recite this while leaving home for Allah's protection. (Sunan Abu Dawood)

In Times of Difficulty

حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

Sufficient for us is Allah, and He is the best disposer of affairs.

This is a dua for reliance on Allah in hardship. (Sahih Bukhari)

Morning Dua

اللَّهُمَّ بِكَ أَصْبَحْنَا وَبِكَ أَمْسَيْنَا

O Allah, by You we reach the morning and by You we reach the evening.

A dua for the morning. (Sunan Abu Dawood)

Evening Dua

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ

O Allah, You are my Lord; there is no deity except You.

A dua for the evening. (Tirmidhi)

Dua for Forgiveness

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَارْحَمْنِي

My Lord, forgive me and have mercy upon me.

A dua for seeking forgiveness. (Sahih Muslim)

Dua for Guidance

اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي وَسَدِّدْنِي

O Allah , guide me and make me steadfast.

A dua for seeking guidance. (Sahih Muslim)
